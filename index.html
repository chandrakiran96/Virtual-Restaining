Introduction

Breast cancer is the leading cause of death within female population.

Histological examination of stained human tissue samples is a definitive approach for diagnosing disease.

The aim is to convert Haematoxylin and Eosin (HE) stained slide images to Immunohistochemistry (IHC) stained images by the use of Generative Adver- sarial Networks (GAN)

Method

Our method involves the Stain density mappings which are obtained from Stain Deconvolution.

Hematoxylin (H), Eosin (E) density mappings were obtained from HE stained image and Hematoxylin (H), Diaminobenzidine (DAB) were obtained from IHC stained images.
